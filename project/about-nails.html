Measuring Tape

You can measure you're nails using a flexible measuring tape with the "mm" side in two ways:

1. Use a regular clear tape placed in the natural nail and mark the widest part of the nail usually the half-way of your nail. Mark right above the sidewall or past the sidewall for a more comfy fitting. Peel the tape off and place on a paper to measure in "mm".

2. Use the measuring tape and directly lay it on the natural nail. Find the size counting every "mm" from sidewall to sidewall.

Note:

Please make sure you are confident with the sizing. If you are unsure, size up. You are responsible for the sizes you provide.No refunds or exchanges will be accepted after order has been started. I use the Apres brand nail tips, which may be consistent with other nail artists.
