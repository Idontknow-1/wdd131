La Mera Mera Nails

The name is incorporating my maiden last name as a saying in Mexico, which means "the main boss, or the highest-ranking, most influential, and skilled woman in a group or field". In this case, for nail art.

Optional domain availability: lamerameranails.com

Purpose

The site will contain information in separate pages like business and owner purpose and story, pricing for custom press on nails and how to find sizes, an album of previous work, and a form for more information.
